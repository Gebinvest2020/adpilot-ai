# AdPilot AI

AI SaaS platform for Google Ads marketers and agencies.

---

## Vision

Build a premium AI-powered SaaS that helps marketers:
- generate Google Ads RSA ads
- improve CTR
- avoid Google Ads moderation issues
- optimize campaigns with AI

Target:
- CIS marketers
- agencies
- freelancers
- small businesses
- Google Ads specialists

Languages:
- English
- Russian
